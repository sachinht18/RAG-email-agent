# Cold email rules for SDR outreach

- Start with a specific account signal, not a generic compliment.
- Keep the body concise: roughly 75 to 120 words.
- One clear idea per sentence is better than stacking multiple claims.
- Move in this order:
  1. relevant opener
  2. pain or change the buyer is likely dealing with
  3. differentiated value proposition
  4. proof or concrete capability
  5. low-friction CTA
- Avoid hype words like "revolutionary", "best-in-class", or "game-changing".
- Do not ask more than one question in the email.
- The CTA should feel easy to decline, not like a hard close.
- If you use a metric, it must be documented and attributable.
